lost things

Rememberings

I lost my wallet in September. It was important to me. Not because of the look or feel of the 50 euros that were still in there, because these very meaningful little memories were captured. One for each of my family. Because they meant a lot to me, I tried to recreate them and archive them here.

My father recently got a speeding ticket, for driving at 180km/h on his way to Nieuwvliet, Netherlands.

My brother was sick and we ordered something from Zollstocker Grill Cologne. This was our order and it was delicious.

My sister and I at a Photobox at Neumarkt Cologne, many years ago. (we made cool signs with our hands)

My mother's press card from 2011. It always fascinates me that she gets one every year, even though she is no longer a member. This reminds me that people can be bold in some situations.
